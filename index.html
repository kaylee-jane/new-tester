What this analysis measures
This instrument evaluates the structural readiness of your organisation to absorb new hires. It assesses role clarity, operational infrastructure, leadership capacity, decision authority, and financial preparedness.

Why this structural signal matters
When a new employee enters an organisation, they introduce new decision pathways, communication flows, and operational dependencies. If internal structure is not prepared to absorb these changes, hiring can create more operational pressure than relief.

What your responses will be used to evaluate
Your responses are analysed to identify key structural risks, decision ownership gaps, and areas of operational fragility. The output is a diagnostic report that pinpoints where to strengthen systems before or alongside scaling headcount.
